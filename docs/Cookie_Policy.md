## 1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how Fleet Feast ("we," "us," "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (https://fleetfeast.com) and related services. This policy should be read together with our [Privacy Policy](Privacy_Policy.md).

**What are cookies?**
Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your actions and preferences over time, improving your browsing experience.

## 2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:
- **Keep you logged in** during your session
- **Remember your preferences** (e.g., language, location)
- **Analyze how you use** our platform to improve functionality

- **Provide personalized content** and recommendations

## 5. Cookie Consent Management

### 5.1 Your Choices

**Essential Cookies:**
- Cannot be disabled as they are necessary for platform functionality
- Blocking these will prevent you from using Fleet Feast

**Optional Cookies (Analytics, Functional, Marketing):**
- You can accept or reject these when you first visit our site
- You can change your preferences at any time via the Cookie Preference Center

### 5.2 How to Manage Cookie Preferences

**Option 1: Cookie Preference Center**
- Click "Cookie Preferences" link in the footer
- Toggle categories on/off (except Essential cookies)
- Click "Save Preferences"
- Changes take effect immediately

**Option 2: Browser Settings**
- Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings
- See browser-specific instructions below
- **Note:** Blocking all cookies may impair platform functionality

### 5.3 Browser Cookie Controls

**Google Chrome:**
1. Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
2. Choose: "Block all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies"
3. Manage exceptions for specific sites

**Mozilla Firefox:**
1. Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose: "Block cookies" or "Delete cookies when Firefox closes"
3. Manage exceptions

**Safari (Mac):**
1. Safari → Preferences → Privacy
2. Choose: "Block all cookies" or "Prevent cross-site tracking"
3. Manage website data

**Microsoft Edge:**
1. Settings → Privacy, search, and services
2. Choose: "Block all cookies" or "Block third-party cookies"
3. Manage site permissions

**Mobile Browsers:**
- iOS Safari: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
- Android Chrome: Settings → Site Settings → Cookies

## 13. Glossary

**Cookie:** Small text file stored on your device by a website.

**First-Party Cookie:** Set by Fleet Feast directly (e.g., `next-auth.session-token`).

**Third-Party Cookie:** Set by external services (e.g., Google Analytics, Stripe).

**Session Cookie:** Temporary cookie deleted when you close your browser.

**Persistent Cookie:** Cookie that remains on your device until expiration or deletion.

**Tracking Pixel:** Tiny invisible image used to track email opens or page views.

**Local Storage:** Browser storage mechanism (not a cookie, but similar privacy implications).

**GDPR:** General Data Protection Regulation (EU privacy law).

**PECR:** Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (UK cookie law).

**CCPA:** California Consumer Privacy Act (California privacy law).
