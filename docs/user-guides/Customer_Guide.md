## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit [fleetfeast.com](https://fleetfeast.com) and click **Sign Up**
2. Choose **I'm a Customer** when prompted for account type
3. Fill in your details:
   - **Email address**: Use a valid email you check regularly
   - **Password**: Must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, and numbers
   - **Confirm password**: Re-enter your password
4. Check the box to agree to our Terms of Service
5. Click **Create Account**

### Verifying Your Email

You'll receive a verification email within minutes:

1. Check your inbox for an email from Fleet Feast
2. Click the **Verify Email** button in the email
3. You'll be redirected to your dashboard
4. If you don't receive the email:
   - Check your spam/junk folder
   - Click **Resend Verification Email** on the login page

### Setting Up Your Profile

Make your booking experience smoother by completing your profile:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Settings**
2. Add your information:
   - **Full Name**: For booking confirmations
   - **Phone Number** (optional): For important updates
   - **Default Event Location**: Your most common event address
3. Click **Save Changes**

1. Click **Find Trucks** in the main navigation
2. Use the search bar to enter:

   - Cuisine type (e.g., "Mexican", "BBQ")

**By Price Range**

**By Event Type**
- Corporate Events
- Weddings
- Birthday Parties

**By Guest Capacity**
- Enter minimum and maximum guest count

## Booking a Food Truck

### Submitting a Booking Request

1. On the truck's profile page, click **Request Booking**
2. Fill in your event details:

**Event Date and Time**
- Select your event date from the calendar
- Choose start time (when you want the truck to arrive)
- Choose end time (when service should conclude)

**Event Location**
- Enter the full street address
- Include any special instructions (parking lot location, building entrance)

**Guest Count**
- Enter expected number of guests
- Be as accurate as possible for proper food preparation

**Event Type**
- Select: Corporate, Wedding, Birthday, Festival, or Other
- This helps the vendor prepare appropriately

**Special Requests** (optional)
- Dietary restrictions to accommodate
- Menu customization requests
- Setup preferences
- Any other important details

3. Review your request details
4. Click **Submit Booking Request**

### Understanding the Request Process

**After You Submit:**
- The vendor receives your booking request notification
- They have **48 hours** to accept or decline
- You'll receive an email notification of their decision

**If Accepted:**
- You'll receive a payment link via email
- Payment must be completed within 24 hours to confirm the booking
- See [Making Payments](#making-payments) section

**If Declined:**
- You'll receive a notification explaining why (if vendor provided a reason)
- Common reasons: unavailable, outside service area, capacity mismatch
- You can immediately search for another truck

### Requesting Custom Quotes

For complex events or when you want to compare multiple options:

1. Click **Request Quote** instead of **Request Booking**
2. Fill in detailed event requirements
3. Send the same request to multiple trucks (up to 5)
4. Compare quotes when vendors respond
5. Choose your preferred vendor and proceed to booking

### Communicating with Vendors

Once your booking request is submitted, you can message the vendor:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Messages**
2. Find the conversation for your booking
3. Type your message and click **Send**

**Important Rules:**
- All communication MUST happen in-app
- Do not share phone numbers or email addresses
- Sharing contact information violates our terms and may result in account suspension
- Vendors cannot see your contact info until the booking is confirmed

**What to Discuss:**
- Menu details and customization
- Setup requirements
- Parking and power needs
- Timeline questions
- Special accommodations

## Making Payments

### Payment Process

When a vendor accepts your booking:

1. You'll receive an email with a payment link
2. Click **Complete Payment** in the email or dashboard
3. You'll be taken to the secure payment page

### Supported Payment Methods

- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- Debit cards
- Digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay)

**Note:** All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Fleet Feast never stores your full card details.

### Understanding the Deposit

**How It Works:**
- You pay the full booking amount upfront
- Fleet Feast holds the payment in escrow
- The vendor receives payment 7 days after your event
- This protects both you and the vendor

**Payment Breakdown:**
- **Base Service Fee**: Quoted by vendor
- **Platform Fee**: Included in the price (no surprise charges)
- **Total Amount**: What you pay

### Loyalty Discount

If you've previously booked the same vendor:
- You'll automatically receive a **5% discount**
- The discount is applied at checkout
- Look for the "Loyal Customer" badge on your booking

### Payment Confirmation

After successful payment:
- You'll receive a confirmation email
- The booking appears as "Confirmed" in your dashboard
- The vendor is notified to prepare for your event

## Cancellation Policy

### Customer Cancellations

We understand plans change. Here's our refund policy:

**7+ Days Before Event**
- **100% refund** of your payment
- No penalties or fees
- Simply cancel through your dashboard

**3-6 Days Before Event**
- **50% refund** of your payment
- Partial compensation to vendor for committed preparation

**Less Than 3 Days Before Event**
- **No refund** available
- Vendor has already purchased supplies and reserved the date

### How to Cancel

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Bookings**
2. Find the booking you want to cancel
3. Click **Cancel Booking**
4. Confirm your cancellation
5. Refund (if applicable) processes within 5-7 business days

### If a Vendor Cancels

If your vendor cancels:

**You Have Two Options:**

1. **Accept a Replacement**
   - Fleet Feast will find a similar truck for your date
   - We'll present options within 24 hours
   - No additional cost if you accept

2. **Receive a Full Refund**
   - 100% refund regardless of timing
   - Plus a $100 credit toward your next booking (if vendor cancels within 3 days of event)

**Vendor Penalties:**
- Vendors face financial penalties for cancellations
- Frequent cancellations result in suspension
- You're protected by our platform guarantee

## During Your Event

### Day-of Coordination

**Vendor Arrival:**
- Expect the vendor 30-60 minutes before service time
- They need time for setup
- Provide parking access and power if needed

**Setup Requirements:**
- Most trucks need a 20x10 foot space
- Access to electrical outlet (if available)
- Clear path for truck to drive in/out

### Contacting Your Vendor

Use the in-app messaging for day-of communication:
- Setup questions
- Timeline adjustments
- Menu changes (if possible)

**For Emergencies:**
- If the vendor is more than 30 minutes late, message them in-app
- If no response, contact Fleet Feast support immediately

### What to Expect

**Quality Service:**
- Friendly, professional vendor
- Clean, licensed food truck
- Food served at proper temperatures
- Setup and breakdown handled by vendor

**Common Issues:**
- **Minor delays** (< 30 min): Normal, traffic happens
- **Significant delays** (> 30 min): Eligible for partial refund
- **No-show**: Full refund + $100 credit (vendor faces $500 penalty)

## After Your Event

### Leaving a Review

Help other customers by sharing your experience:

1. You'll receive a review prompt email 1-2 days after your event
2. Go to **Dashboard** → **Reviews**
3. Find your completed booking
4. Click **Write Review**

**What to Include:**
- **Star Rating** (1-5 stars)
  - 5 stars: Excellent, exceeded expectations
  - 4 stars: Good, met expectations
  - 3 stars: Okay, some room for improvement
  - 2 stars: Below expectations
  - 1 star: Poor experience

- **Written Review**
  - Food quality and taste
  - Professionalism and service
  - Timeliness and setup
  - Overall experience
  - Would you book again?

**Review Guidelines:**
- Be honest but respectful
- Focus on facts, not emotions
- Mention specific highlights or issues
- Help future customers make informed decisions

**Note:** Only customers with completed bookings can leave reviews. This ensures authenticity.

### Reporting Issues

If something went wrong:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Bookings**
2. Find the completed booking
3. Click **Report Issue**
4. Select issue type:
   - Vendor no-show
   - Late arrival (specify how late)
   - Food quality problems
   - Wrong menu items
   - Quantity issues
   - Other concerns

5. Provide detailed description with:
   - What happened
   - When it occurred
   - How it affected your event
   - Photos if applicable

6. Submit the report

### Dispute Resolution

**Automated Resolutions:**
Some issues are resolved automatically:
- **Vendor no-show**: Full refund + $100 credit
- **Late 30-60 minutes**: 10% refund
- **Late 60+ minutes**: 25% refund

**Manual Review:**
Complex issues are reviewed by our support team:
- Food quality complaints
- Menu discrepancies
- Quantity disputes

**Resolution Timeline:**
- Disputes must be filed within 7 days of your event
- Automated resolutions: Immediate
- Manual reviews: 3-5 business days
- You'll be notified of the outcome via email

### Payment Release

If no issues are reported:
- Vendor receives payment 7 days after your event
- This protects you during the dispute window
- No action needed from you

   - **Pending**: Awaiting vendor response

1. On any truck profile, click the **heart icon**

### Payment History

View all your transactions:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Payments**
2. See all charges and refunds
3. Download receipts for your records
4. Filter by date or status

### Notification Preferences

Customize what emails you receive:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Settings** → **Notifications**
2. Toggle preferences:
   - **Booking Updates**: When vendors respond to requests
   - **Payment Confirmations**: Receipt emails
   - **Event Reminders**: 24 hours before your event
   - **Messages**: When vendors message you
   - **Marketing**: Promotional offers and new trucks

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Settings** → **Profile**
2. Update your information:
   - Name

To permanently delete your account:

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Settings** → **Account**

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### I can't log in

**Forgot password:**
1. Click **Forgot Password** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for reset instructions
4. Create a new password

**Email not verified:**
1. Check for the verification email (check spam)
2. Click **Resend Verification Email**
3. Verify your email before logging in

**Account locked:**
- Too many failed login attempts may temporarily lock your account
- Wait 15 minutes and try again
- Contact support if issue persists

#### My booking request wasn't accepted

**Common reasons:**
- Vendor is already booked on that date
- Your event is outside their service area
- Guest count doesn't match their capacity
- Vendor doesn't serve your event type

**What to do:**
- Search for alternative trucks
- Adjust your event details if flexible
- Request custom quotes from multiple vendors

#### I'm not receiving emails

**Check these:**
1. Verify your email address in settings
2. Check spam/junk folder
3. Add noreply@fleetfeast.com to your contacts
4. Check notification preferences are enabled

**Still not working:**
- Contact support with your account email
- We can verify delivery status

#### The truck was late to my event

**During the event:**
- Message the vendor in-app for status update
- If 30+ minutes late with no communication, contact support

**After the event:**
- File a dispute within 7 days
- Late arrivals of 30-60 minutes qualify for 10% refund
- Late arrivals of 60+ minutes qualify for 25% refund

#### I need to change my booking details

**Before vendor accepts:**
- Cancel the request and submit a new one with correct details

**After vendor accepts:**
- Message the vendor in-app to request changes
- Minor changes (guest count adjustments, menu tweaks) are usually accommodated
- Major changes may require rebooking

**Date/time changes:**
- Check vendor availability for new date
- May require cancellation and rebooking with applicable cancellation fees

#### I was charged the wrong amount

1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Payments**
2. View the payment breakdown
3. Verify the charge matches the quote
4. If incorrect:
   - Click **Report Payment Issue**
   - Provide details and expected amount
   - Support will investigate within 2 business days

#### I haven't received my refund

**Refund timeline:**
- Refunds process within 5-7 business days
- May take additional 3-5 days to appear in your account (depending on your bank)

**If it's been over 2 weeks:**
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Payments**
2. Click **Refund Status** on the cancelled booking
3. Contact support if status shows "Completed" but you haven't received it

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much does it cost to book a food truck?**
A: Prices vary by vendor, menu, and guest count. Most range from $500-$2,000. You'll see exact quotes before booking.

**Q: Is there a platform fee?**
A: Platform fees are included in the vendor's quoted price. What you see is what you pay.

**Q: Can I book multiple trucks for one event?**
A: Currently, each booking is for one truck. To book multiple trucks, submit separate booking requests for each.

**Q: What if it rains on my event day?**
A: Most food trucks operate in light rain. Discuss weather contingencies with your vendor via in-app messaging.

**Q: Can I customize the menu?**
A: Many vendors offer menu customization. Discuss your needs in the booking request special requests field or via messaging.

**Q: Do food trucks serve alcohol?**
A: Some trucks have liquor licenses. Check the truck profile or ask the vendor directly.

**Q: What if I need to cancel last minute?**
A: Cancellations within 3 days of the event are non-refundable. We recommend event insurance for added protection.

**Q: Can I tip my vendor?**
A: Yes! You can add a tip when you leave a review, or tip in cash on the day of your event.

**Q: Are food trucks licensed and insured?**
A: All Fleet Feast vendors are verified to have current business licenses, health permits, and liability insurance.

**Q: What's the service area?**
A: Currently, Fleet Feast operates in New York City and surrounding areas. Check individual vendor profiles for their specific service areas.
