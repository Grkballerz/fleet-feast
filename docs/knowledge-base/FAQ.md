# Frequently Asked Questions

## For Customers

### Account & Registration

**Q: How do I create an account?**

A: Creating an account is quick and easy:
1. Visit the Fleet Feast homepage
2. Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email, create a password, and provide your name
4. Verify your email by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile with location preferences

**Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**

A: To reset your password:
1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Select "Forgot Password?"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if needed)
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Log in with your new password

**Q: Can I update my account information?**

A: Yes! Go to Settings > Profile to update your name, email, location, and preferences.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**

A: To delete your account:
1. Go to Settings > Account
2. Select "Delete Account"
3. Confirm deletion (note: this action cannot be undone)
4. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days

### Finding Food Trucks

**Q: How do I search for food trucks in my area?**

A: Use the search functionality to find food trucks:
1. Enter your location or allow location access
2. Browse the map view to see nearby food trucks
3. Use filters to narrow results by cuisine type, date, price range, or ratings
4. View vendor profiles to see menus, photos, and reviews

**Q: Can I filter by cuisine type?**

A: Yes! We support filtering by multiple cuisine types including:
- American
- Mexican
- Asian
- Italian
- BBQ
- Desserts
- Vegetarian/Vegan
- And many more!

**Q: How do I know if a food truck is available on my date?**

A: Each vendor's profile shows their calendar availability. When you select a date, only available vendors will appear in search results.

**Q: Can I view menus and pricing before booking?**

A: Absolutely! Each vendor profile includes:
- Full menu with descriptions
- Pricing information
- Sample photos
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Dietary information (allergens, vegan options, etc.)

### Booking

**Q: How far in advance can I book?**

A: You can book food trucks up to 6 months in advance. Some popular vendors may have limited availability during peak seasons, so booking early is recommended.

**Q: What happens if a vendor doesn't respond to my request?**

A: Vendors have 48 hours to accept or decline booking requests. If they don't respond within this window, the request automatically expires and you'll be notified. No charges will be made.

**Q: How does the cancellation policy work?**

A: Our cancellation policy is tiered based on timing:
- **7+ days before event**: 100% refund
- **3-6 days before event**: 50% refund
- **Less than 3 days before event**: No refund

Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

**Q: Can I modify my booking after it's confirmed?**

A: Yes, you can request modifications by contacting the vendor directly through the in-app messaging system. Changes are subject to vendor approval and may affect pricing.

**Q: What information do I need to provide when booking?**

A: You'll need to provide:
- Event date and time
- Event location (with address)
- Expected number of guests
- Event type (private party, corporate event, wedding, etc.)
- Any special requirements or dietary restrictions

**Q: Do I need to provide electricity or water hookups?**

A: Requirements vary by vendor. Check the vendor's profile for their specific needs. Some food trucks are self-sufficient, while others may require electricity, water, or both.

### Payments

**Q: What payment methods are accepted?**

A: We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through our secure Stripe payment processor. We do not currently accept PayPal or cash.

**Q: When am I charged?**

A: You are charged after the vendor accepts your booking request. A pending authorization may appear on your card when you submit the request, but the actual charge only occurs upon vendor confirmation.

**Q: Is there a deposit required?**

A: Most bookings require a 50% deposit at the time of confirmation, with the remaining balance due 72 hours before the event. Some vendors may have different deposit requirements.

**Q: How do refunds work?**

A: Refunds are processed based on our cancellation policy:
- Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method
- Processing time is 5-10 business days
- You'll receive email confirmation when the refund is initiated

**Q: Are there any additional fees?**

A: Fleet Feast charges a small service fee (typically 5-8% of the booking total) to cover platform costs. This fee is clearly displayed before you complete your booking. There are no hidden fees.

**Q: Can I get an invoice or receipt?**

A: Yes! After your booking is confirmed, you'll receive:
- Immediate booking confirmation email
- Invoice with full payment breakdown
- Receipt after the event is completed

All documents are also available in your account under "My Bookings."

### Reviews & Ratings

**Q: Can I leave a review after my event?**

A: Yes! We encourage all customers to leave honest reviews. After your event, you'll receive an email invitation to rate and review your experience. Reviews help other customers make informed decisions.

**Q: What if I have a complaint about a vendor?**

A: If you have concerns:
1. Contact the vendor directly through our messaging system
2. If unresolved, contact Fleet Feast support with your booking ID
3. We'll investigate and mediate if necessary
4. Serious violations may result in vendor suspension

**Q: Can vendors see my review before I post it?**

A: No, reviews are published immediately and cannot be edited by vendors. However, vendors can respond to reviews publicly.

## For Vendors

### Getting Started

**Q: How do I become a vendor on Fleet Feast?**

A: The application process is straightforward:
1. Click "Become a Vendor" on our homepage
2. Complete the vendor application form
3. Upload required documents (see below)
4. Wait for approval (typically 3-5 business days)
5. Set up your vendor profile and calendar
6. Start receiving booking requests!

**Q: What documents do I need to apply?**

A: Required documents include:
- Valid business license
- Food handler's permit/certification
- Proof of liability insurance (minimum $1 million coverage)
- Vehicle registration and inspection certificate
- Tax ID/EIN
- Photos of your food truck (exterior and interior)
- Sample menu

**Q: Is there a fee to join as a vendor?**

A: There's no upfront fee to join Fleet Feast. We only charge a platform fee on completed bookings (see payment section below).

**Q: How long does the approval process take?**

A: Most applications are reviewed within 3-5 business days. You'll receive email updates on your application status. If additional documentation is needed, we'll contact you directly.

**Q: Do I need insurance?**

A: Yes, all vendors must maintain:
- General liability insurance (minimum $1 million)
- Commercial auto insurance
- Workers' compensation (if you have employees)

Insurance certificates must be current and on file.

### Managing Bookings

**Q: How long do I have to respond to booking requests?**

A: You have 48 hours to accept or decline booking requests. If you don't respond within this window, the request automatically expires. We recommend responding as quickly as possible to secure bookings.

**Q: Can I decline a booking request?**

A: Yes, you can decline requests if:
- You're already booked for that time
- The event requirements don't match your capabilities
- The location is outside your service area

However, frequent declines may affect your vendor rating.

**Q: How do I update my availability calendar?**

A: To manage your calendar:
1. Log into your vendor dashboard
2. Navigate to "Calendar"
3. Block out unavailable dates
4. Set your operating hours
5. Mark special events or holidays
6. Save changes

Your calendar automatically updates on your public profile.

**Q: Can I set minimum booking requirements?**

A: Yes! You can set:
- Minimum guest count
- Minimum booking fee
- Lead time requirements (e.g., "2 weeks advance notice")
- Service area restrictions (max distance from base)

**Q: What if I need to cancel a confirmed booking?**

A: Vendor-initiated cancellations are serious and should be avoided when possible. If you must cancel:
1. Contact the customer immediately through the platform
2. Provide a valid reason
3. Fleet Feast support will handle the customer refund
4. Note: Frequent cancellations may result in account suspension

### Payments & Fees

**Q: What is the platform fee?**

A: Fleet Feast charges a tiered platform fee:
- **Standard rate**: 15% of the booking total
- **Loyalty rate**: 10% for vendors who achieve Silver tier (50+ completed bookings)

This fee covers payment processing, marketing, customer support, and platform maintenance.

**Q: When do I get paid?**

A: Payments are processed as follows:
- Deposit: Held in escrow until 7 days after event completion
- Final payment: Released 7 days after the event date
- Direct deposit to your bank account
- Payment statements available in your vendor dashboard

**Q: What if a customer doesn't show up?**

A: If you arrive at the event location and the customer is not present:
1. Document your arrival with timestamped photos
2. Attempt to contact the customer through the platform
3. Wait 30 minutes past the scheduled start time
4. Report the no-show to Fleet Feast support
5. You'll receive full payment for the booking

**Q: Can I offer discounts or promotions?**

A: Yes! You can create:
- Seasonal promotions
- First-time customer discounts
- Loyalty rewards
- Last-minute booking discounts

All promotions are managed through your vendor dashboard.

**Q: How do I update my banking information?**

A: To update payment details:
1. Go to Vendor Dashboard > Settings > Payment Information
2. Enter your new bank account details
3. Verify with micro-deposits (if required)
4. Save changes

Changes take effect for your next payout.

### Profile & Marketing

**Q: How can I improve my visibility on the platform?**

A: To boost your profile:
- Maintain high ratings (4.5+ stars)
- Upload high-quality photos
- Keep your menu current and detailed
- Respond quickly to booking requests
- Maintain an active calendar
- Collect positive reviews from customers
- Participate in platform promotions

**Q: Can I customize my vendor profile?**

A: Absolutely! You can customize:
- Banner image and logo
- Business description
- Menu items with photos and prices
- Service area and travel preferences
- Specialty tags (vegan, organic, gluten-free, etc.)
- Social media links

**Q: How do customer reviews affect my listing?**

A: Reviews directly impact:
- Your overall star rating
- Search ranking
- Customer trust and booking rate
- Eligibility for featured placements

Always strive to provide excellent service!

- Chrome (recommended)

## Safety & Trust

**Q: How does Fleet Feast verify vendors?**

A: All vendors undergo:
- Document verification (licenses, permits, insurance)
- Background checks for business owners
- Initial quality review of food truck and menu
- Ongoing monitoring of reviews and performance
- Annual recertification requirements

**Q: What if I feel unsafe at an event?**

A: Your safety is our priority. If you feel unsafe:
- Leave the situation immediately
- Contact local authorities if necessary
- Report the incident to Fleet Feast support
- We'll investigate and take appropriate action

**Q: Can I see vendor ratings before booking?**

A: Yes! All vendor profiles display:
- Overall star rating
- Number of reviews
- Response time
- Completion rate
- Years on platform

**Q: What is Fleet Feast's dispute resolution process?**

A: If there's a dispute:
1. Contact the other party through our messaging system
2. Document the issue with photos/details
3. If unresolved, escalate to Fleet Feast support
4. Our mediation team will review evidence from both parties
5. We'll make a fair determination within 5-7 business days
6. Decisions may include partial refunds, future credits, or other remedies
