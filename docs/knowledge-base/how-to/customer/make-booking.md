# How to Make a Booking

Booking a food truck for your event is a straightforward process. This guide will walk you through every step.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- A Fleet Feast account (see [How to Create an Account](./create-account.md))
- Selected a food truck vendor (see [How to Search for Food Trucks](./search-food-trucks.md))
- Event details ready (date, time, location, guest count)
- A valid payment method

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Navigate to the Vendor's Profile

- Find your chosen vendor through search
- Click on their profile to view full details
- Review their menu, pricing, and requirements
- Check their cancellation policy

### 2. Click "Request Booking"

- Locate the "Request Booking" button on the vendor profile
- You'll be taken to the booking request form

**Note**: If you're not logged in, you'll be prompted to log in or create an account first.

### 3. Fill Out Event Details

Complete all required fields:

**Event Information:**
- **Event Date**: Select from the calendar (only available dates shown)
- **Event Time**: Choose start time (use vendor's available hours)
- **Event Duration**: Estimate how long you need the truck (e.g., 3 hours)
- **Event Type**: Select from dropdown
  - Birthday Party
  - Wedding
  - Corporate Event
  - Festival
  - Private Party
  - Community Event
  - Other

**Location Details:**
- **Street Address**: Full event venue address
- **City, State, ZIP**: Complete location details
- **Venue Type**: (Private residence, park, office, etc.)
- **Parking Instructions**: Where the truck should set up
- **Access Notes**: Gate codes, loading dock info, etc.

**Guest Information:**
- **Number of Guests**: Your best estimate
- **Expected Serving Window**: When guests will be eating
- **Service Style**: (Individual orders, pre-set menu, buffet style)

### 4. Specify Menu Preferences

**Menu Selection:**
- Browse the vendor's menu
- Select items you're interested in
- Indicate if you want:
  - Full menu availability
  - Limited menu selection
  - Custom package (if offered)

**Dietary Requirements:**
- Note any allergies or dietary restrictions
- Specify vegetarian/vegan guest count
- Request gluten-free or other special options

**Additional Requests:**
- Special menu items (if vendor offers customization)
- Beverage preferences
- Dessert or appetizer options

### 5. Review Requirements and Services

Check what the vendor needs:

**Vendor Requirements:**
- Power supply (if needed): Note voltage and outlet type
- Water hookup (if needed): Availability at your venue
- Setup space: Dimensions required (usually 15-20 feet)
- Parking access: Can the truck access your venue?
- Setup time: How early they need to arrive

**Additional Services:**
- Staffing: Number of vendor staff included
- Equipment: Tables, tents, trash removal
- Duration: Covered serving time
- Cleanup: What's included

### 6. Add Special Instructions

Use the "Special Instructions" text box for:
- Event theme or special requests
- Timeline requirements
- Coordination with other vendors
- Specific presentation needs
- Any unique circumstances

**Example:**
```
This is a surprise 50th birthday party. Please arrive by 5:30 PM and
keep the truck hidden until 6:00 PM. We'd like the truck positioned
near the back patio. There will be other vendors (bartender and DJ),
so coordination appreciated.
```

### 7. Review Pricing

The booking form will show:

**Base Pricing:**
- Price per person OR flat event fee
- Guest count × per person rate
- Minimum booking fee (if applicable)

**Additional Costs:**
- Travel fee (if outside vendor's primary area)
- Setup/breakdown fees
- After-hours surcharge (if applicable)
- Weekend/holiday premium (if applicable)
- Special equipment rental

**Platform Fees:**
- Fleet Feast service fee (typically 5-8%)
- Payment processing fee

**Total Estimate:**
- Subtotal before fees
- All fees itemized
- **Total Booking Amount**

### 8. Review Deposit and Payment Terms

Understand the payment structure:

**Deposit:**
- Typically 50% of total at booking
- Charged when vendor accepts your request
- Held until 7 days after event

**Final Payment:**
- Remaining balance due 72 hours before event
- Automatic charge to your payment method
- Confirmation email sent

**Cancellation Policy:**
- 7+ days before: 100% refund
- 3-6 days before: 50% refund
- Less than 3 days: No refund

### 9. Add Payment Method

**New Users:**
- Click "Add Payment Method"
- Enter credit/debit card details
- Billing address
- Save card for future bookings (optional)

**Returning Users:**
- Select saved payment method
- Or add a new card

**Security:**
- All payments processed through Stripe (PCI compliant)
- We never store your full card number
- SSL encrypted transactions

### 10. Review and Submit

**Final Checklist:**
- [ ] Event date, time, and location correct
- [ ] Guest count accurate
- [ ] Menu selections finalized
- [ ] Special requirements noted
- [ ] Payment method added
- [ ] Total cost reviewed
- [ ] Cancellation policy understood

**Submit Your Request:**
- Review the booking summary
- Check the "I agree to the terms and conditions" box
- Click "Submit Booking Request"

### 11. Wait for Vendor Response

After submitting:

**Immediate Confirmation:**
- You'll see a confirmation screen
- Booking request number assigned
- Email confirmation sent

**Pending Status:**
- Your request shows as "Pending" in your dashboard
- Vendor has 48 hours to respond
- You'll receive notifications about status changes

**Possible Outcomes:**

1. **Accepted**:
   - Vendor accepts your booking
   - Deposit is charged
   - Booking confirmed
   - You receive detailed confirmation email

2. **Declined**:
   - Vendor is unavailable
   - Request automatically expires
   - No charge to your card
   - You can search for alternative vendors

3. **Counteroffer**:
   - Vendor suggests modifications (price, time, menu)
   - You can accept, decline, or negotiate
   - Original request stays pending

4. **Expired**:
   - No response within 48 hours
   - Request automatically closes
   - No charge to your card

### 12. After Booking is Confirmed

Once accepted:

**Confirmation Details:**
- Booking ID and confirmation number
- Complete vendor contact information
- Final itinerary and timeline
- Invoice with payment breakdown

**Communication:**
- In-app messaging with vendor
- Coordinate final details
- Make any last-minute adjustments

**Preparations:**
- Ensure venue access on event date
- Confirm power/water availability (if required)
- Prepare parking/setup area
- Share any updates with vendor

## Tips for Successful Bookings

### Increase Your Chances of Acceptance

1. **Provide Complete Information**: Detailed requests get faster responses
2. **Be Flexible**: Offer alternative dates if possible
3. **Realistic Guest Counts**: Accurate numbers help vendors plan
4. **Clear Communication**: Be specific about your needs
5. **Book Early**: Popular vendors fill up quickly
6. **Meet Minimums**: Respect vendor minimum booking requirements

### Common Booking Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Inaccurate Guest Count**: Can affect food quantity and pricing
❌ **Incomplete Address**: May delay vendor response
❌ **Unrealistic Timeline**: Give vendors adequate setup time
❌ **Ignoring Requirements**: Vendor needs power/water but venue doesn't have it
❌ **Last-Minute Booking**: Many vendors require advance notice
❌ **Unclear Instructions**: Leads to confusion on event day

### Booking Different Event Types

**Private Parties:**
- Guest count: 20-100 typically
- Duration: 2-4 hours
- Best for: Birthdays, anniversaries, celebrations

**Corporate Events:**
- Guest count: 50-500+
- Duration: 2-6 hours
- May need: Multiple trucks, formal invoicing, contracts

**Weddings:**
- Guest count: 75-300+
- Duration: 4-6 hours
- Special considerations: Menu tasting, formal service, backup plans

**Festivals/Public Events:**
- Guest count: Variable (500-5000+)
- Duration: Full day or multi-day
- Considerations: Permits, multiple vendors, high volume

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Submit Booking Request

**Check:**
- All required fields completed (marked with *)
- Valid payment method added
- Venue address is complete
- Guest count meets vendor minimum
- Selected date is available

### Payment Method Declined

**Try:**
- Verify card details are correct
- Check card has sufficient funds
- Contact your bank about the charge
- Try a different card
- Contact support if issue persists

### Vendor Hasn't Responded

**Remember:**
- Vendors have 48 hours to respond
- Check your spam folder for notifications
- View request status in your dashboard
- Request expires after 48 hours if no response
- You can send a polite message via platform

### Need to Modify Request

**Before Acceptance:**
- You can cancel and resubmit with changes
- Or message vendor to discuss modifications

**After Acceptance:**
- Contact vendor through messaging system
- Changes subject to vendor approval
- May affect pricing or terms

## After Your Event

### Leave a Review

Help other customers by:
- Rating the vendor (1-5 stars)
- Writing detailed review
- Uploading photos
- Highlighting what went well

### Request Invoice/Receipt

- Available in "My Bookings" section
- Download PDF for your records
- Email copy to yourself
- Use for expense reimbursement

### Report Issues

If something went wrong:
- Document with photos/notes
- Contact vendor first to resolve
- Escalate to Fleet Feast support if needed
- Include booking ID in all communications
