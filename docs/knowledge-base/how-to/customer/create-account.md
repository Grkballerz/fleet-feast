# How to Create an Account

Creating an account on Fleet Feast gives you access to book food trucks, save favorites, and track your booking history.

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address
- Internet connection
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Navigate to the Fleet Feast Homepage

- Open your web browser
- Go to `www.fleetfeast.com`

### 2. Click the Sign Up Button

- Look for the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
- Click it to open the registration form

### 3. Fill Out the Registration Form

Enter the following information:

**Required Fields:**
- **Email Address**: Use a valid email you can access
- **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters, including uppercase, lowercase, and numbers)
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password to confirm
- **First Name**: Your given name
- **Last Name**: Your family name

**Optional Fields:**
- **Phone Number**: For booking notifications
- **Location**: Your city or ZIP code (helps us show nearby food trucks)

### 4. Review Terms and Privacy Policy

- Check the box to agree to our Terms of Service
- Check the box to agree to our Privacy Policy
- Optionally, subscribe to our newsletter for deals and updates

### 5. Click "Create Account"

- Click the "Create Account" button at the bottom of the form
- You'll see a confirmation message

### 6. Verify Your Email

- Check your email inbox for a verification message from Fleet Feast
- **Tip**: If you don't see it within 5 minutes, check your spam/junk folder
- Click the "Verify Email" button or link in the email
- You'll be redirected to the Fleet Feast confirmation page

### 7. Complete Your Profile (Optional but Recommended)

After verification, you'll be prompted to:

- **Add a Profile Photo**: Upload a picture (optional)
- **Set Location Preferences**: Add your primary location for personalized results
- **Choose Cuisine Preferences**: Select your favorite food types
- **Enable Notifications**: Choose how you want to receive updates (email, SMS, app)

### 8. Start Exploring!

Once your account is set up, you can:
- Search for food trucks in your area
- Save favorite vendors
- Make booking requests
- View your booking history

## Troubleshooting

### Email Verification Not Received

If you don't receive the verification email:

1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Verify you entered the correct email address
3. Wait 10 minutes and request a new verification email
4. Contact support if still not received

### Password Requirements Not Met

Your password must include:
- At least 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- Special characters are recommended but optional

### "Email Already Registered" Error

If you see this error:
- You may already have an account
- Try logging in instead
- Use the "Forgot Password" feature if needed
- Contact support if you believe this is an error
