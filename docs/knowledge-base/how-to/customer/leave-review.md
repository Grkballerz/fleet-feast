# How to Leave a Review

Reviews help other customers make informed decisions and help vendors improve their service. Your honest feedback is valuable!

## Why Reviews Matter

**For Other Customers:**
- Discover quality vendors
- Learn about real experiences
- Make confident booking decisions
- Understand what to expect

**For Vendors:**
- Build reputation and credibility
- Improve services based on feedback
- Attract more bookings
- Showcase their strengths

**For Fleet Feast:**
- Maintain platform quality
- Identify top performers
- Address issues quickly
- Improve user experience

## When You Can Leave a Review

**Timing:**
- Reviews open after your event date has passed
- Available within 24 hours of event completion
- Window stays open for 30 days
- After 30 days, review option closes

**Eligibility:**
- Only customers who completed a booking can review
- One review per booking
- Cannot review cancelled bookings
- Cannot review vendors you haven't booked

## Step-by-Step: Writing a Review

### 1. Access the Review Form

**Option A: From Email Invitation**
- Check your email 24 hours after event
- Look for "Review Your Experience" email
- Click "Write Review" button
- Opens directly to review form

**Option B: From Your Account**
- Log in to Fleet Feast
- Go to "My Bookings"
- Find the completed booking
- Click "Write Review" button

**Option C: From Vendor Profile**
- Visit the vendor's profile page
- If you've booked them, "Write Review" appears
- Click to open review form

### 2. Rate the Vendor (Required)

**Overall Rating:**
- Select 1-5 stars
- 5 stars = Excellent
- 4 stars = Good
- 3 stars = Average
- 2 stars = Poor
- 1 star = Very Poor

**Category Ratings (Optional but Recommended):**

Rate each aspect separately (1-5 stars):

- **Food Quality**: Taste, freshness, presentation
- **Service**: Friendliness, professionalism, responsiveness
- **Timeliness**: Arrival time, setup, readiness
- **Value**: Quality vs. price paid
- **Communication**: Pre-event coordination
- **Cleanliness**: Truck appearance, food handling

**Why Rate Categories:**
- Helps vendors know specific strengths/weaknesses
- Helps customers evaluate what matters most to them
- More detailed than overall rating alone

### 3. Write Your Review (Required)

**Title (Optional):**
- Short, catchy summary
- Example: "Perfect for Our Wedding!"
- Example: "Great Food, Professional Service"

**Review Body (Required, 50-1000 characters):**

**What to Include:**
- Specific details about your experience
- What went well
- What could be improved
- Food items you tried
- How vendor handled special requests
- Overall impression

**Be Constructive:**
- Honest but respectful
- Specific examples, not just general statements
- Balanced perspective (pros and cons)
- Helpful for future customers

**Example Good Review:**
```
We booked [Vendor] for our company picnic with 100 guests.
The tacos were delicious - especially the al pastor and
carnitas. The vendor arrived early, set up quickly, and the
staff was incredibly friendly. Lines moved fast even with a
large crowd. Only minor issue was they ran out of chips
toward the end, but they handled it professionally. Highly
recommend for large events!
```

**Example Poor Review (Don't Do This):**
```
Food was bad. Service was terrible. Would not recommend.
```
*Too vague, not helpful to others*

### 4. Upload Photos (Optional but Encouraged)

**Why Add Photos:**
- Show actual food presentation
- Provide visual proof of quality
- Help others see what to expect
- Increase review credibility

**How to Upload:**
1. Click "Add Photos" button
2. Select up to 5 photos from your device
3. Drag to reorder if desired
4. Add captions (optional)

**Photo Tips:**
✅ Well-lit, clear images
✅ Food close-ups
✅ Truck setup at your event
✅ Presentation and serving
✅ Crowd enjoying the food

❌ Blurry or dark photos
❌ Unrelated images
❌ Inappropriate content
❌ Photos of people without permission

**Photo Requirements:**
- File format: JPG, PNG, HEIC
- Max size: 10MB per photo
- Max photos: 5 per review
- Content must be appropriate and relevant

### 5. Add Tags (Optional)

Select applicable tags to help categorize your review:

**Event Type:**
- Wedding
- Birthday Party
- Corporate Event
- Festival
- Private Party

**Highlights:**
- Great Value
- Fast Service
- Delicious Food
- Professional Staff
- Beautiful Presentation
- Accommodating
- Punctual

**Good For:**
- Large Groups
- Kids
- Dietary Restrictions
- Last-Minute Bookings
- Outdoor Events

### 6. Review Guidelines Check

Before submitting, ensure your review:

**✅ DO:**
- Base review on your actual experience
- Be honest and balanced
- Include specific details
- Respect privacy (no personal info)
- Focus on food and service
- Use appropriate language

**❌ DON'T:**
- Include personal contact information
- Use profanity or offensive language
- Make threats or accusations
- Discuss pricing disputes (contact support instead)
- Include spam or promotional content
- Post fake or fraudulent reviews
- Violate vendor's privacy

### 7. Preview and Submit

**Preview Your Review:**
- Click "Preview" to see how it will appear
- Check for typos or errors
- Ensure photos uploaded correctly
- Verify rating is accurate

**Make Edits:**
- Click "Edit" to make changes
- Update text, ratings, or photos
- Preview again

**Submit:**
- Click "Submit Review"
- Confirmation message appears
- Email confirmation sent
- Review published immediately

## After You Submit

### Your Review is Live

**Immediate Publishing:**
- Review appears on vendor's profile instantly
- Visible to all Fleet Feast users
- Included in vendor's average rating
- Shows your name and event date

**Review Display:**
```
★★★★★ 5.0
Jane D. | Corporate Event | June 2025

"Perfect for Our Wedding!"

We booked [Vendor] for our company picnic with 100 guests...
[rest of review]

[Photos if uploaded]

Helpful? 👍 15   👎 2
```

### Vendor Response

**Vendors Can Respond:**
- Vendors may reply to your review
- Response appears below your review
- Both review and response are public
- You'll be notified via email if they respond

**Example Vendor Response:**
```
Vendor Response:
"Thank you so much for the kind words, Jane! We loved being
part of your company picnic. We apologize about running out
of chips - we've since adjusted our inventory planning for
large events. Hope to serve you again!"
```

### Edit or Delete Your Review

**Can You Edit?**
- ✅ Yes, within 30 days of posting
- ✅ Edit text, photos, ratings
- ✅ Changes publish immediately
- ❌ Cannot edit after 30 days

**How to Edit:**
1. Go to "My Reviews" in your account
2. Find the review you want to edit
3. Click "Edit Review"
4. Make changes
5. Click "Update Review"

**Can You Delete?**
- ✅ Yes, anytime
- ⚠️ Deletion is permanent
- Vendor's average rating recalculates

**How to Delete:**
1. Go to "My Reviews"
2. Find the review
3. Click "Delete"
4. Confirm deletion

### Report Vendor Response

If a vendor's response is:
- Inappropriate or offensive
- Contains personal attacks
- Violates terms of service

**Report It:**
1. Click "Report Response" link
2. Select reason
3. Add details
4. Submit report
5. We'll review within 24-48 hours

## Review Moderation

### Fleet Feast Review Policy

**We May Remove Reviews That:**
- Contain hate speech or discrimination
- Include personal information
- Are spam or promotional
- Are proven false or fraudulent
- Violate our Community Guidelines
- Contain illegal content

**We Do NOT Remove Reviews:**
- Just because they're negative
- At vendor's request (unless violates policy)
- For expressing honest opinions
- For describing real experiences

### Dispute a Review Removal

If your review is removed:
1. You'll receive email notification
2. Explanation provided
3. You can appeal within 14 days
4. Email: reviews@fleetfeast.com
5. Include review details and reasoning

## Review Best Practices

### Writing Helpful Reviews

**Be Specific:**
❌ "Food was good"
✅ "The pulled pork was tender and flavorful, the coleslaw was fresh and tangy"

**Provide Context:**
❌ "Great vendor"
✅ "Perfect for our 50-person outdoor wedding - handled the crowd efficiently"

**Balance Positive and Negative:**
❌ "Everything was perfect!"
✅ "Food was amazing, but they arrived 15 minutes late. However, they made up time during setup."

**Include Actionable Feedback:**
❌ "Service was slow"
✅ "Consider having 2 staff members for events over 75 people to speed up service"

### What Makes Reviews Helpful?

Fleet Feast users can mark reviews as "helpful" or "not helpful"

**Helpful Reviews:**
- Detailed and specific
- Include both pros and cons
- Describe the event context
- Provide useful information
- Well-written and clear

**Less Helpful Reviews:**
- Too short or vague
- Only emotional reactions
- No useful details
- Off-topic content
- Unclear or confusing

## Review Impact

### How Reviews Affect Vendors

**Positive Reviews (4-5 stars):**
- Improve search ranking
- Increase booking requests
- Build trust and credibility
- Qualify for featured placements
- Unlock loyalty program benefits

**Negative Reviews (1-3 stars):**
- Lower search ranking
- Decrease booking conversion
- Flag for quality review
- May require improvement plan
- Can lead to account restrictions if pattern emerges

**Vendor Rating Tiers:**
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8+: Top Rated (featured in searches)
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.0-4.7: Good Standing
- ⭐⭐⭐ 3.0-3.9: Needs Improvement (monitored)
- ⭐⭐ Below 3.0: Under Review (restricted)

### Review Statistics

Customers can view:
- **Total Reviews**: Number of reviews vendor has
- **Average Rating**: Overall star average
- **Rating Distribution**: Breakdown by star count (5★:85%, 4★:10%, etc.)
- **Category Averages**: Food quality, service, value, etc.
- **Response Rate**: % of reviews vendor responded to
- **Recent Trend**: Rating over last 3 months

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How soon after my event can I leave a review?**
A: Within 24 hours of your event date. The review window stays open for 30 days.

**Q: Can I review a vendor I cancelled?**
A: No, only completed bookings can be reviewed.

**Q: Can I leave multiple reviews for the same vendor?**
A: Yes, if you've booked them multiple times. One review per booking.

**Q: Can I review anonymously?**
A: No, your name (first name + last initial) appears with your review. This ensures authenticity.

**Q: Will my review affect the vendor's payment?**
A: No, vendors receive payment 7 days after the event regardless of reviews. However, consistent poor reviews may lead to account restrictions.

**Q: Can a vendor sue me for a negative review?**
A: Honest reviews based on your experience are protected. Stick to facts, avoid defamation, and you're legally protected.

**Q: What if the vendor contacts me privately to change my review?**
A: Report this to Fleet Feast immediately at reviews@fleetfeast.com. This violates our policies.

**Q: Can I get compensated for writing a review?**
A: Fleet Feast occasionally runs campaigns rewarding reviews, but vendors cannot offer incentives for positive reviews.
