# How to Search for Food Trucks

Finding the perfect food truck for your event is easy with Fleet Feast's powerful search and filtering tools.

## Overview

Fleet Feast allows you to search for food trucks by:
- Location (map or address search)
- Date and time availability
- Cuisine type
- Price range
- Ratings and reviews
- Special dietary options

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### 1. Access the Search Page

**Option A: From Homepage**
- Go to `www.fleetfeast.com`
- The search bar is prominently displayed on the homepage

**Option B: From Navigation Menu**
- Click "Find Food Trucks" in the main navigation menu

### 2. Enter Your Location

You have three options for setting location:

**Option A: Use Current Location**
- Click the "Use My Location" button
- Allow location access when prompted by your browser
- The map will center on your current position

**Option B: Enter an Address**
- Type your event address in the "Location" field
- Select from the autocomplete suggestions
- The map will center on the selected address

**Option C: Enter City or ZIP Code**
- Type your city name or ZIP code
- Select from the dropdown options
- Browse trucks in that general area

### 3. Select Your Event Date and Time

- Click the "Date" field to open the calendar picker
- Select your event date
- Choose your event start time from the dropdown
- Choose your event duration (optional but recommended)

**Note**: Only food trucks available on your selected date will appear in results.

### 4. Apply Filters (Optional)

Refine your search using these filters:

**Cuisine Type**
- Click "Cuisine Type" dropdown
- Select one or more options:
  - American
  - Mexican
  - Asian
  - Italian
  - BBQ
  - Desserts
  - Vegetarian/Vegan
  - And more!

**Price Range**
- Use the slider to set your budget
- Prices shown are estimates per person or per event
- Options: $, $$, $$$, $$$$

**Dietary Options**
- Filter by special dietary needs:
  - Vegetarian
  - Vegan
  - Gluten-Free
  - Nut-Free
  - Dairy-Free
  - Halal
  - Kosher

**Vendor Ratings**
- Filter by minimum star rating
- Options: 3+ stars, 4+ stars, 4.5+ stars

**Additional Filters**
- New vendors (joined in last 3 months)
- Featured vendors
- Loyalty program participants
- Self-sufficient (no power/water needed)

### 5. Browse Search Results

Results appear in two views:

**Map View** (Default)
- Food truck locations shown as pins on the map
- Click a pin to see basic vendor info
- Cluster markers show multiple vendors in the same area

**List View**
- Click "List View" to switch
- Vendors displayed as cards showing:
  - Vendor name and logo
  - Cuisine type
  - Star rating and review count
  - Price range
  - Distance from your location
  - Sample photos

**Sort Results**
- Best Match (default)
- Highest Rated
- Most Reviews
- Price: Low to High
- Price: High to Low
- Distance: Nearest First

### 6. View Vendor Profiles

Click on any vendor card to view their full profile:

**Profile Includes:**
- Vendor photos (truck exterior and food)
- About section (vendor story and specialties)
- Full menu with prices
- Availability calendar
- Customer reviews and ratings
- Location and service area
- Equipment requirements (power, water)
- Booking policies and minimum requirements

### 7. Add to Favorites (Optional)

- Click the heart icon on any vendor card
- Vendors are saved to "My Favorites" for easy access
- Access favorites from your account dashboard

### 8. Refine Your Search

Not finding what you're looking for?

**Expand Search Area**
- Increase the distance radius using the map zoom controls
- Some vendors travel longer distances for larger events

**Adjust Filters**
- Remove or modify filters to see more results
- Try broader cuisine categories
- Adjust your price range

**Change Date/Time**
- Try alternative dates if your event is flexible
- Popular vendors book up quickly, especially weekends

### 9. Compare Vendors

Use the compare feature to evaluate options side-by-side:

1. Check the boxes on up to 3 vendor cards
2. Click "Compare Selected" button
3. View comparison table showing:
   - Pricing
   - Ratings
   - Menu highlights
   - Availability
   - Special features

### 10. Request a Booking

Once you've found your perfect vendor:

1. Click "Request Booking" on their profile
2. Fill out the booking request form
3. Submit for vendor review

**See**: [How to Make a Booking](./make-booking.md) for detailed instructions.

## Search Tips

### Get Better Results

1. **Be Flexible with Dates**: Popular vendors may be booked on your first choice date
2. **Book Early**: Many top vendors book 2-3 months in advance for peak seasons
3. **Read Reviews**: Look for recent reviews and vendor responses
4. **Check Menus Carefully**: Ensure the vendor offers what you're looking for
5. **Consider Travel Distance**: Vendors may charge travel fees for distant locations

### Use Advanced Search

Click "Advanced Search" to access additional options:
- Multiple cuisine types (AND/OR logic)
- Specific menu items or keywords
- Vendor experience level (years in business)
- Event type specialization (weddings, corporate, festivals)
- Setup requirements and constraints

### Save Your Searches

Create a free account to:
- Save search criteria for repeated use
- Set up alerts for new vendors matching your preferences
- Receive notifications when your favorite vendors have openings

## Mobile Search

Fleet Feast is fully mobile-optimized:

**On Mobile Devices:**
- Use the app for on-the-go searching
- Filter results with simplified mobile interface
- Use GPS for accurate location-based results
- Call vendors directly from their profiles
- Access map navigation to vendor locations

## Troubleshooting

### No Results Found

If your search returns no results:

1. **Expand Location Radius**: Increase the search distance
2. **Remove Filters**: Some filter combinations may be too restrictive
3. **Try Different Dates**: Your selected date may have no availability
4. **Contact Support**: We can help find vendors that match your needs

### Map Not Loading

If the map doesn't display:

1. Check your internet connection
2. Enable JavaScript in your browser
3. Allow location access if using "current location"
4. Try refreshing the page
5. Clear your browser cache
6. Try a different browser

### Inaccurate Location Results

If location results seem wrong:

1. Re-enter your address more specifically (include street number)
2. Use ZIP code instead of city name
3. Manually adjust the map to your desired area
4. Clear location permissions and re-grant them
