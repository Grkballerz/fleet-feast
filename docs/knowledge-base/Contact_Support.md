## What to Include When Contacting Support

### Essential Information

**Always Provide**:
- Your email address (used for account)
- Your full name
- Nature of the issue (brief summary)

**If About a Booking**:
- Booking ID (found in "My Bookings")
- Vendor name
- Event date
- Brief description of the issue

**If About Payment**:
- Transaction ID (from payment history)
- Booking ID
- Amount in question
- Date of transaction
- Last 4 digits of card used

**If Technical Issue**:
- Browser name and version (Chrome 98, Safari 15, etc.)
- Device type (iPhone 13, Windows laptop, etc.)
- Operating system (iOS 15, Windows 11, etc.)
- What you were trying to do
- Error message (exact wording or screenshot)
- Steps to reproduce the issue

**If Vendor Application**:
- Application ID
- Date submitted
- Business name
- Specific question or concern

### Helpful Additions

**Screenshots**:
- Show error messages
- Display what you're seeing
- Highlight the problem area
- Include timestamp if relevant

**Documentation**:
- Booking confirmations
- Email correspondence
- Payment receipts
- Relevant contracts or agreements

**Timeline**:
- When issue started
- Previous attempts to resolve
- Any error messages received
- When you need resolution by (if time-sensitive)

## Support Ticket Process

### How It Works

**1. You Submit**:
- Contact via any channel
- Provide information
- Describe issue clearly

**2. We Receive**:
- Ticket automatically created
- Assigned ticket number
- Routed to appropriate team

**3. We Acknowledge**:
- Confirmation email sent
- Ticket number provided
- Expected response time given

**4. We Investigate**:
- Review your information
- Check account details
- Research the issue
- Gather necessary data

**5. We Respond**:
- Email with findings
- Request more info if needed
- Provide solution or next steps

**6. You Reply** (if needed):
- Answer our questions
- Provide additional info
- Confirm resolution

**7. We Resolve**:
- Issue fixed or answered
- Ticket closed
- Follow-up email sent

### Tracking Your Ticket

**By Email**:
- Check ticket number in subject line
- Example: [Ticket #12345]
- All correspondence threads together

**In Dashboard**:
- Go to Settings → Support Tickets
- View all open and closed tickets
- See status and history
- Add updates to existing tickets

**Status Meanings**:
- **Open**: We're working on it
- **Waiting for Customer**: We need more info from you
- **In Progress**: Being actively resolved
- **Resolved**: Issue fixed
- **Closed**: Ticket complete

## Escalation Process

### When to Escalate

**Escalate If**:
- No response after 48 hours
- Issue not resolved after multiple attempts
- Support representative unable to help
- Time-sensitive issue not being prioritized
- Unsatisfied with resolution

### How to Escalate

**Step 1**: Reply to original ticket
- Reference ticket number
- Explain why escalating
- Provide additional context

**Step 2**: Request supervisor/manager
- Be polite but firm
- Explain urgency
- Outline previous attempts

**Step 3**: Email executive support
- **Email**: escalations@fleetfeast.com
- Include all previous correspondence
- Summarize issue clearly
- Explain impact and urgency

**Step 4**: Social media (last resort)
- Public tweet or post (professional tone)
- Tag @FleetFeast
- Briefly describe unresolved issue
- Usually prompts faster response

## Frequently Asked Questions About Support

**Q: How long until I get a response?**
A: Email: 24-48 hours. Chat/Phone: Immediate during business hours. Emergency: 1-2 hours.

**Q: Can I call outside business hours?**
A: Emergency hotline (day-of-event issues) operates 6 AM - 11 PM EST daily. Standard support is business hours only.

**Q: What if my issue isn't resolved?**
A: Escalate through escalations@fleetfeast.com with your ticket number and explanation.

**Q: Do you charge for support?**
A: No, all support is free for Fleet Feast users.

**Q: Can I request a specific representative?**
A: We'll try to accommodate, but can't guarantee. High-volume customers get dedicated reps.

**Q: How do I compliment great service?**
A: Reply to your support email with praise, fill out satisfaction survey, or email feedback@fleetfeast.com.

**Q: What if I disagree with a decision?**
A: Request escalation, explain your reasoning, provide any new information. Decisions can be reviewed.
