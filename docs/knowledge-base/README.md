## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Customers

**Get the Best Results**:
- Book early for popular dates (2-3 months ahead for weddings)
- Be flexible with dates for better availability
- Read vendor reviews carefully
- Communicate clearly about event details
- Provide accurate guest counts
- Ask questions before booking

**Save Money**:
- Book weekday events (often lower rates)
- Consider off-season dates
- Compare multiple vendors
- Look for new vendor promotions
- Book during non-peak hours

### For Vendors

**Maximize Bookings**:
- Complete your profile 100%
- Upload 15+ high-quality photos
- Respond to requests within 2 hours
- Maintain 4.5+ star rating
- Keep calendar updated daily
- Offer competitive pricing

**Increase Earnings**:
- Reach 50 bookings for loyalty tier (10% vs 15% fee)
- Upsell add-ons and extras
- Expand your service area
- Target corporate events (higher average value)
- Optimize your pricing strategy
- Build repeat customer base

### Customer Protection

- Fraud protection

### Vendor Protection

- Payment guaranteed after event
- Clear cancellation policies
- Customer verification
- Platform mediation for disputes
- Insurance required from customers for large events
