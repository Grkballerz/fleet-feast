# Troubleshooting Guide

Common issues and their solutions for Fleet Feast customers and vendors.

## For Customers

### Account Issues

#### Cannot Log In

**Symptoms**: Incorrect password error, account not found

**Solutions**:
1. **Reset Password**:
   - Click "Forgot Password" on login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Check email for reset link (check spam folder)
   - Create new password
   - Try logging in again

2. **Verify Email Address**:
   - Ensure you're using the correct email
   - Check for typos
   - Try alternate email addresses you may have used

3. **Clear Browser Cache**:
   - Clear cookies and cache
   - Close and reopen browser
   - Try logging in again

4. **Try Different Browser**:
   - Use Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge
   - Try incognito/private mode
   - Update browser to latest version

5. **Account Locked**:
   - Too many failed login attempts locks account for 30 minutes
   - Wait and try again
   - Or contact support to unlock immediately

**Still Can't Log In?** [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md)

---

#### Email Not Verified

**Symptoms**: "Please verify your email" message, can't complete registration

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Email Inbox**:
   - Look for email from support@fleetfeast.com
   - Subject: "Verify Your Fleet Feast Account"
   - Check spam/junk folder

2. **Request New Verification Email**:
   - Click "Resend Verification Email" on login page
   - Enter your email address
   - Check inbox again (wait up to 10 minutes)

3. **Add to Safe Senders**:
   - Add support@fleetfeast.com to contacts
   - Mark as "Not Spam" if in junk folder
   - Request verification email again

4. **Check Email Filters**:
   - Corporate email may have strict filters
   - Contact IT department if applicable
   - Try personal email instead

**Still Not Receiving Email?** Contact support with your email address

---

#### Account Locked or Suspended

**Symptoms**: "Account suspended" message when trying to log in

**Possible Reasons**:
- Multiple failed payment attempts
- Terms of service violation
- Fraudulent activity suspected
- Multiple chargebacks

**Solutions**:
1. Check email for suspension notice
2. Review reason for suspension
3. [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) to appeal or resolve
4. Provide requested documentation
5. Wait for account review (typically 24-48 hours)

### Search and Booking Issues

#### No Search Results Found

**Symptoms**: "No vendors found" message after searching

**Solutions**:
1. **Expand Search Radius**:
   - Increase distance in filters
   - Some vendors travel further for larger events
   - Check map view for nearby options

2. **Remove Filters**:
   - Too many filters may be too restrictive
   - Remove cuisine type, price range, or other filters
   - Search again with fewer restrictions

3. **Try Different Date**:
   - Your selected date may have no availability
   - Try weekdays instead of weekends
   - Check one week earlier or later

4. **Verify Location**:
   - Re-enter your address or ZIP code
   - Use city name instead of specific address
   - Allow location access for accurate results

5. **Browse All Vendors**:
   - Click "View All Vendors"
   - Filter manually
   - Contact vendors directly about availability

**Tips**:
- Book further in advance (many vendors book months ahead)
- Be flexible with dates
- Consider alternative cuisine types

---

#### Can't Submit Booking Request

**Symptoms**: Form won't submit, error messages appear

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Required Fields**:
   - All fields marked with * must be completed
   - Guest count must be entered
   - Event date must be selected
   - Location must be complete

2. **Verify Payment Method**:
   - Credit card must be added
   - Card must not be expired
   - Billing address must match card
   - Try different card if one fails

3. **Guest Count Issues**:
   - Ensure you meet vendor's minimum
   - Check maximum capacity
   - Enter a realistic number

4. **Date Not Available**:
   - Selected date may have just been booked
   - Refresh page and try again
   - Select alternative date

5. **Technical Issues**:
   - Refresh the page
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try different browser
   - Disable browser extensions
   - Try on mobile device

**Still Can't Submit?** [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) with booking details

---

#### Vendor Not Responding to Request

**Symptoms**: Booking request pending for over 24 hours, no response from vendor

**Understanding**:
- Vendors have 48 hours to respond
- Request automatically expires after 48 hours
- You won't be charged if vendor doesn't respond

**Solutions**:
1. **Wait the Full 48 Hours**:
   - Many vendors respond within 12-24 hours
   - Be patient, especially if request sent on weekend

2. **Check Request Status**:
   - Go to "My Bookings"
   - View request status
   - Look for vendor messages

3. **Send Polite Follow-Up Message**:
   - Use in-app messaging
   - Ask if they received request
   - Provide additional details if needed

4. **If Request Expires**:
   - No charge to your card
   - Search for alternative vendors
   - Try vendor again with different date

**Prevent This**:
- Book vendors with fast response time badge
- Check vendor's average response time on profile
- Book further in advance

### Payment Issues

#### Payment Declined

**Symptoms**: "Payment failed" error, card declined message

**Common Reasons**:
- Insufficient funds
- Incorrect card details
- Card expired
- Bank fraud protection triggered
- International card restrictions

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify Card Details**:
   - Check card number is correct
   - Verify expiration date (MM/YY)
   - Ensure CVV code is correct
   - Confirm billing address matches bank records

2. **Check with Your Bank**:
   - Ensure sufficient funds available
   - Verify card allows online purchases
   - Check for fraud alerts/blocks
   - Ask bank to authorize the charge

3. **Try Different Card**:
   - Use alternative credit/debit card
   - Avoid prepaid cards (often declined)
   - Business cards sometimes have restrictions

4. **International Cards**:
   - Notify bank of US purchase
   - Ensure card works internationally
   - Try different card issued in US

5. **Technical Issue**:
   - Wait 15 minutes and try again
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try different browser
   - Use mobile app instead

**Still Declined?** Contact your bank first, then [our support](./Contact_Support.md)

---

#### Refund Not Received

**Symptoms**: Booking cancelled, refund approved, but money not in account

**Understanding**:
- Refunds take 5-10 business days
- Goes to original payment method
- Shows as "pending" before posting

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Timing**:
   - Allow full 5-10 business days
   - Business days exclude weekends/holidays
   - Be patient

2. **Verify Original Card**:
   - Refund goes to card used for booking
   - Check that specific card's statement
   - If card was closed, contact bank

3. **Check Fleet Feast Account**:
   - Go to "My Bookings" → "Payment History"
   - Verify refund shows as "Completed"
   - Note the refund date

4. **Contact Your Bank**:
   - Ask them to trace the refund
   - Provide transaction ID from Fleet Feast
   - They can locate pending refunds

5. **If 10+ Business Days**:
   - [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md)
   - Provide booking ID
   - Include bank statement showing no refund
   - We'll investigate and re-issue if needed

---

#### Charged Wrong Amount

**Symptoms**: Charged more/less than expected, duplicate charge

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify Booking Total**:
   - Go to "My Bookings"
   - View booking details
   - Check final invoice
   - Compare to card statement

2. **Check for Modifications**:
   - Booking changes affect price
   - Guest count adjustments
   - Menu modifications
   - Extra services added

3. **Understand Fee Structure**:
   - Fleet Feast service fee (5-8%)
   - Two charges: deposit (50%) + final payment (50%)
   - Total = booking cost + fees

4. **Authorization vs. Charge**:
   - Initial authorization may show different amount
   - Actual charge posts after vendor accepts
   - Authorization drop-off once charge posts

5. **Duplicate Charge**:
   - Rare but possible
   - Check if both are posted (not one pending)
   - [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) immediately
   - Include both transaction IDs

**Dispute Process**:
- Email: payments@fleetfeast.com
- Include booking ID and transaction details
- We'll investigate within 24-48 hours
- Refund issued if error confirmed

### Technical Issues

#### Page Won't Load / App Crashing

**Solutions**:
1. **Check Internet Connection**:
   - Verify WiFi or data is working
   - Try loading other websites
   - Restart router if needed

2. **Clear Cache and Cookies**:
   - Browser: Settings → Clear Browsing Data
   - Mobile: App Settings → Clear Cache
   - Restart browser/app

3. **Update Browser/App**:
   - Use latest version
   - Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge supported
   - Update mobile app from app store

4. **Try Different Device**:
   - Use computer instead of phone
   - Try tablet
   - Test on different network

5. **Disable Extensions**:
   - Browser extensions may interfere
   - Try incognito/private mode
   - Disable ad blockers temporarily

6. **Check System Status**:
   - Visit status.fleetfeast.com
   - Check for known outages
   - See estimated resolution time

**Persistent Issues?** [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) with browser/device details

---

#### Images Not Loading

**Solutions**:
1. Allow images in browser settings
2. Disable data saver mode
3. Clear browser cache
4. Check firewall/security settings
5. Try different browser

---

#### Map Not Displaying

**Solutions**:
1. Enable JavaScript in browser
2. Allow location access
3. Clear browser cache
4. Disable browser extensions
5. Try different browser
6. Check if Google Maps works on other sites

## For Vendors

### Account and Profile Issues

#### Application Pending Too Long

**Normal Timeline**: 3-5 business days

**If Longer**:
1. Check email for information requests
2. Log in and check application status
3. Verify all documents were uploaded
4. [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) after 7 business days

---

#### Can't Upload Documents

**Solutions**:
1. **Check File Format**:
   - Accepted: PDF, JPG, PNG
   - Max size: 10MB per file
   - Ensure not corrupted

2. **Reduce File Size**:
   - Compress PDFs online
   - Resize images
   - Use lower resolution

3. **Try Different File**:
   - Scan document again
   - Save in different format
   - Take new photo

4. **Browser Issues**:
   - Try different browser
   - Disable extensions
   - Clear cache

---

#### Profile Not Appearing in Searches

**Common Reasons**:
1. **Calendar Not Available**:
   - Check you have open dates
   - Update calendar availability
   - Unblock dates

2. **Incomplete Profile**:
   - Add minimum 10 photos
   - Complete all required fields
   - Upload menu with pricing

3. **Service Area Mismatch**:
   - Customer location outside your area
   - Expand service area if desired
   - Update in profile settings

4. **Low Rating**:
   - Ratings below 3.0 limit visibility
   - Focus on improving service
   - Address negative reviews

5. **Account Suspended**:
   - Check for suspension notice
   - Review terms of service
   - Contact support to resolve

### Booking and Calendar Issues

#### Double-Booked / Calendar Conflict

**Prevention**:
1. Always update calendar immediately after booking
2. Block dates for external commitments
3. Use calendar sync if available

**If It Happens**:
1. Contact both customers IMMEDIATELY
2. Explain situation honestly
3. Help find replacement vendor for one booking
4. Offer significant future discount
5. Document everything
6. Report to support

**Consequences**:
- May affect vendor rating
- Customer may leave negative review
- Platform fee not refunded
- Repeat violations = suspension

### Payment Issues

#### Payout Not Received

**Normal Timeline**: 7 days after event + 1-3 days for bank deposit

**Solutions**:
1. **Verify Timing**:
   - Count 7 full days from event date
   - Add 1-3 business days
   - Check if still within window

2. **Check Bank Account**:
   - Verify correct account on file
   - Look for pending deposits
   - Contact bank to trace payment

3. **Review Payout Status**:
   - Dashboard → Earnings → Pending Payouts
   - Check for "Released" status
   - Look for error messages

4. **Bank Account Issues**:
   - Account closed or frozen?
   - Routing/account number incorrect?
   - Update banking information
   - Contact support to re-issue

**After 10+ Days**: [Contact Support](./Contact_Support.md) with booking ID

---

#### Incorrect Payout Amount

**Verify**:
1. Check booking total
2. Confirm platform fee (15% or 10% loyalty)
3. Look for adjustments or refunds
4. Review customer modifications

**If Still Incorrect**:
- Email: vendors@fleetfeast.com
- Include booking ID
- Show expected vs. actual calculation
- Allow 24-48 hours for investigation

---

#### Bank Verification Failed

**Solutions**:
1. **Re-check Information**:
   - Routing number (9 digits)
   - Account number
   - Account type (checking/savings)

2. **Contact Your Bank**:
   - Verify account is active
   - Confirm account accepts ACH deposits
   - Get correct routing number

3. **Look for Micro-Deposits**:
   - Check account for 2 small deposits (under $1)
   - Enter amounts on verification page
   - May take 2-3 business days to appear

4. **Try Different Account**:
   - Use checking instead of savings
   - Ensure account is in your business name
   - Avoid recently opened accounts
