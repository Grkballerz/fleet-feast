## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:
- Create an account (customer or vendor)
- Make a booking
- Communicate with vendors through our messaging system
- Contact customer support
- Subscribe to marketing communications

**Customer Information:**
- Email address (required)
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Profile information (optional)
- Booking details (event date, location, guest count, special requests)
- Payment information (processed by Stripe, not stored on our servers)

**Vendor Information:**
- Email address (required)
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Business name
- Cuisine type
- Service area and location
- Capacity information (min/max guest count)
- Business documents (licenses, permits, insurance certificates)
- Stripe Connect account information
- Menu details and pricing

### 2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

When you use Fleet Feast, we automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, features used, time spent on platform, clickstream data
- **Location Data:** Approximate location based on IP address (for vendor search)
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies:** See our [Cookie Policy](Cookie_Policy.md) for details

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:
- **Stripe:** Payment processing status, payout information (for vendors)
- **Email Service Providers:** Email delivery status, open rates (SendGrid/AWS SES)
- **Identity Verification Services:** Business verification data (for vendor approval)

## 7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards

- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted via HTTPS/TLS 1.3
- **Encryption at Rest:** Database encryption enabled on hosting provider
- **Password Security:** Passwords hashed using bcrypt (cost factor 12)
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control (ADMIN, VENDOR, CUSTOMER)
- **Secure Authentication:** JWT-based session management via NextAuth.js

### 7.2 Organizational Safeguards

- **Limited Access:** Only authorized personnel access personal data
- **Vendor Management:** Data Processing Agreements with all third parties
- **Regular Audits:** Annual security and compliance audits
- **Employee Training:** Staff trained on data protection practices

### 7.3 PCI DSS Compliance

**Payment Card Security:**
- We do NOT store credit card information on our servers
- All card data processed by Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)
- We comply with PCI DSS SAQ A requirements
- Stripe Elements used for secure card input

### 7.4 Automated Decision-Making

**Message Content Filtering:**
Fleet Feast uses automated systems to detect policy violations in messages (e.g., sharing contact information to circumvent platform fees). This automated decision-making helps maintain platform integrity.

- **How it works:** Messages are scanned for phone numbers, email addresses, and suspicious patterns
- **Impact:** Flagged messages may result in account warnings or suspensions
- **Your rights:** You can appeal automated decisions by contacting support@fleetfeast.com
- **Human review:** All high-severity flags are reviewed by human moderators
